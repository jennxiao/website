Backend Internship @ STRATIM

This summer, I created a mini server support mobile app events independently. The fast-paced development cycle of the start-up meant that most tasks went to one main monolithic server, which was dangerous because it would affect the performance of one of their main products (their app).

I also developed STRATIM internal tooling using Java Play. After the STRATIM was aquired by KAR, registering the new employees into KAR created an HR bottleneck in processing the emoployees as already background checked. Thus, I was tasked with creating a CS workaround: now with a click of a button, the employee would be marked and automatically have access to all the necessary portals.

Overall, my internship showed me how to optimize my workflow and how to learn independently and ask the right questions to the right people.
