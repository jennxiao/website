Food Discovery Mobile App

This app puts the focus of restaurant searching back towards what is important: the food. Through a personalized newsfeed, users can scroll through images of dishes from the local area to decide exactly what they are craving instantly rather than get lost in all of the details about price or restaurant name.

So far, I have designed mockups, performed user testing, and iterated on that design. See my designs here

I'm continuing to develop this project under my club, Codeology . As the project leader, I am in charge of a 3 person development team. I am teaching them the fundamentals of UI Design, iOS development, API Calls, REST API framework. At the end of our 8 weeks together, we will have created an app that allows users to swipe through restaurants based on their responses to an NLP chatbot
